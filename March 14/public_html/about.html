About Us

The Public Employment Service Office (PESO) is a non-fee charging multi-employment service facility...

The PESO's are community-based and maintained largely by local government units (LGU's)...

What are the functions of the PESO?

1. Encourage employers to submit job vacancies...

2. Develop and administer testing...

3. Provide persons with entrepreneurship qualities...

4. Undertake employability enhancement training...

5. Provide employment and occupational counseling...

6. Conduct pre-employment counseling...

7. Provide reintegration assistance...

8. Perform such functions as to willfully carry out the objectives of the PESO Act.
